Who We Are

MBP Capital is a student-run global macro fund and investment society at LSE. We give members hands-on experience in macroeconomic research, portfolio management, and investment analysis – skills that are directly applicable to careers in finance, consulting, and beyond.

What We Offer

• A structured Global Macro Bootcamp where you learn our investment framework from the ground up
• Opportunities to conduct live research and contribute to real investment decisions
• Networking events with industry professionals from leading financial institutions
• An annual Stock Pitch Competition in partnership with top firms
• A collaborative community of ambitious, intellectually curious students

How to Get Involved
