Congratulations! On behalf of the Executive Committee of MBP Capital, we are thrilled to inform you that your team has advanced to the Finals of the 2026 MBP Capital x Citadel Global Stock Pitch Competition.

The Finals will be held in person in London on 4th March 2026, where you will present your pitch to our distinguished panel of judges.

Section #1: Administrative and Logistical Requirements

1. Acceptance of Finals Invitation

Please reply to this email by 21st February 2026, 23:59 GMT to formally confirm your team's acceptance of this invitation to compete in the Finals. If we do not receive confirmation by this date, we may need to offer your spot to an alternate team.

2. VISA Requirements for UK Entry

As the Finals will be held in person in London, UK on 4th March 2026, please immediately assess whether any of your team members require a visa to enter the United Kingdom.

⚠️ IMPORTANT VISA INFORMATION:

  • If you require a visa, you must begin the application process immediately. Visa processing times can vary significantly.
  • It is your responsibility to obtain a valid visa to enter the UK by 4th March 2026.
  • Please inform us in your reply email if any team members face visa issues or concerns.
  • We cannot guarantee accommodations for teams unable to secure proper travel documentation.

3. Team Information Form

Please complete the form linked below. This information will be used by Citadel to make arrangements for accommodations and flights for all finalists traveling to London. ALL team members must fill up the form individually.

If you have any queries regarding the form or travel arrangements, please reply all to this email—Marie Treharne from the Citadel team will follow up with any clarifications.

Complete Team Information Form

Section #2: Resubmission of Pitch Materials

Teams that wish to update or refine their pitch memo and/or financial model for the Finals are permitted to do so. Updated materials must be submitted by 25th February 2026, 23:59 GMT.

Please note:

  • Resubmission is optional. You may present with your original materials if you prefer.

  • All resubmissions must still comply with the 2-page maximum for the pitch memo.

  • Late submissions will not be accepted.

If you choose to resubmit materials, please send them to info@mbpcapital.co.uk with the subject line: "MBP Capital x Citadel Stock Pitch Competition - FINALS Resubmission".

Section #3: Finals Presentation Format

The Finals presentation will follow this structure:

📊 Presentation Timing:

  • 10 minutes for your pitch presentation
  • 8 minutes for Q&A with the judges
  • Total time: 18 minutes

⏱️ Hard Cutoffs Will Be Enforced:

To ensure fairness to all competing teams, the time limits will be strictly enforced. You will receive a 1-minute warning before your allotted time expires. If you exceed the 10-minute presentation limit, your presentation will be stopped, and you will proceed directly to Q&A.

Additional Guidelines:

  • The team will send over a follow-up email detailing the specific reporting time and location for the 4th March 2026. The specific programme outline of the competition will be sent over closer to the competition date.

  • Business formal attire is required.

  • Technical support will be available on-site.

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us at info@mbpcapital.co.uk.

Once again, congratulations on this outstanding achievement. We look forward to seeing you compete at the Finals on 4th March 2026!
