The attached rulebook gives you a detailed breakdown of event proceedings and other key information; please revise it thoroughly in advance of the competition. We have highlighted the key points below. Additionally, please note that comments on decorum and etiquette are only listed below:

Presentation

Please ensure you have a maximum of 2 pages for your memo, and that your pitch lasts no more than 10 minutes. In the interest of fairness, this limit will be strictly enforced, and you will be given a 1-minute warning. The judges follow a set rubric and schedule, so your presentation should adhere to those guidelines to be scored appropriately.

Following your pitch, there will be 10 minutes of open Q&A from the judges. Presenting teams will be expected to share their screens during their pitch, so please join the call with your PDF ready to share.

Professional Decorum

  • Please dress in business formal attire, and ensure your background is free of distractions (a sensible virtual background is also permitted).

  • Please change your Zoom name to the following format:

    "Team Name - FirstName LastName"

  • By joining the call, you consent to be photographed and featured on MBP Capital's media platforms. If you have any concerns about this, please reach out to us.

Zoom Logistics

  • The semifinals will be a private event, so you are expected not to share the Zoom link outside your team.

  • If you are not presenting during the 1st slot, you may leave the call until it is your turn to present. Teams must join the call no later than 5 minutes before their scheduled pitch, and absent teams cannot be accommodated.

Finals, Sponsor Panel, and Closing Ceremonies

These events will be hosted in person on the LSE Campus on 4th March 2026.
